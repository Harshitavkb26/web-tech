Gidelines

1. Group Qualifiers
We will make groups consisting of 20 Teams . This will be qualifiers round consisting of only one match(TPP-Erangel) . 4 teams with highe st Points will be selected into next Round.

This Final event will be of 3 Matches(TPP-Erangel, TPP-Miramar, TPP-Sanhok).
