Intro

“If debugging is the process of removing software bugs, then programming must be the process of putting them in.” - Edsger Dijkstra
We invite all programming enthusiasts to participate in online programming contest of IIT Silchar. IOPC is the annual International Online Programming Contest of IIT silchar, organized by the Techtantra team. It is a prestigious programming contest where each coder whacks his brain to code solutions to the given problems. It is the place where optimization and proficiency matters, skills matter, hard work matters, trying till the very end matters and winning loads of cash prizes matter!
There is no need for abstract submissions for Software Street competitions (even if you receive a mail saying so)
This is an online competition. No need to pay the Hospitality fees.
There is no need to worry if you are team status is "pending"

Gidelines

IOPC is an individual rated contest this time. So, make registrations only individual.
Students (undergraduates or graduates) or professionals across the world are eligible to participate.
The contest will be hosted on hackerearth. In order to claim the prize money, you need to register on both platform and Techtantra website.
Only participants who are currently registered as students in a universit y will be eligible for prizes.
