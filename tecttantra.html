About us

Started in 1988 with the motive of encouraging technology, scientific thinking and innovation among the emerging generation of engineers. Techfest is now recognised as Asia's largest Science and Technology festival with a footfall of more that 2,75,000 annually and a reach of over 2560+ Indian colleges and over 600+ colleges abroad. The 33rd edition of the festival will be held from 26th august to 29th august 2020.

A grand 4 day on the beautiful campus of IIT Silchar attracting people from all walks of life including students academics ,proffestional and the general public.Techtantra in the last 32 years has constantly grown and pushed the boundaries of what a college fest can be with something to offer for everyone techtantra is the ideadestination for all technophiles; young or old beginer or expert to wittness,learn and experience the wonders of science and technology
